Hobbies

Astronomy

People have been interested in astronomy since the beginning of time.
The Babylonians developed a calendar based on the sun and the phases of the Moon in the time period from about 1800 to 400 B.C and analyzed the variations in the velocity of the Sun and Moon numerically. Later, the Greeks took a geometrical approach to understand the celestial motions.
Since that time, we have been able to continue studying the vastness that is space. We have been able to learn things such as the fact that the Earth revolves around the sun at a speed of 67 thousand miles per hour and that the sun is moving around the Milky Way at a speed of 828 thousand km an hour and that the Milky Way is moving through space at a speed of 1.4 million miles an hour, and yet none of us are dizzy!

Archery

The bow and arrow are believed to be one of the three most important inventions in human history. The bow and arrow made it possible for our ancestors to hunt bigger prey from a safer distance. It allowed humans to consume a more protein rich diet and gave them more raw materials like bone for tools and hide for warm clothing. There have been cave paintings that date as far back as twelve thousand years ago that depict man hunting with a bow and arrow. Today, archery has evolved as a tool for survival to one that is also used for sport. Archery is a unique and rewarding challenge requiring that archers have endurance and strength to perform well.
